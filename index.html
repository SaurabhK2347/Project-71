school auditorium

was esablished in 1990.

We only get to visit the School Auditorium when we earn it. For example, we have to be quiet in the hallways, we have to turn in our homework on time, and we have to be good students FOR A WEEK to get to go to the Auditorium for 90 mins or 2 hours. When we do earn it, we all get so excited that we are going to the Auditorium. We get Ice Cream as we watch some people perform. We also watch somethings like movies or shows on the screen that drops down in to the Auditorium. It is a very fun place to be because there is no classwork or homework in the Auditorium, just fun!!



school achievements

School vs. School Debate on Air Pollution
won first place in school vs. school debate on air pollution

The Debate lasted for one hour. It was so intense and it felt like we had a tough competition. Which we did. The other team's people had many reasons and evidence to support and backup their side. We also did the same things and we all took turns to go. The opposing team went first and then our team went. This kept on happening back and forth until everyone was done. The principal clapped for everyone and said that she was so proud! A few days later, we got the results for the winning team and it said that we had beat them by 18 points! We were so happy and felt so relieved that everyone was cheering and the teachers threw us a party! The other team did an awesome job too!

School vs. School Soccer Match
won first place in school vs. school soccer match

The Soccer Match lasted forever and everyone was so tired. I was on the defending part of my team where I had to steal the ball and either try to make a goal or pass it to one of my team mates. I scored 1 or 2 goals in the first half. But the +refree blew the whistle and said it was halftime. We all went to go get water and we came up with a plan to win the match. The score was 4-5 and our score was 4. We were losing by one goal. The other team scored another goal and we were now 2 scores behind. One of my friends on my team went to make a goal and he did, but. He hit his leg on the hard ground and got injured. The refree said that he could not play and now we were one player short. The other team was about to score when I took the ball and passed it to my team mate and then he scored. We had to get one more goal in to win. We all knew we had to give everything we have in order to make this goal. One of my friends took the ball and hurried towards the other team's goal, but they stopped him and took the ball from him. He stole it back and kicked. AND HE SCORED!! Everone on our team was so happy that we had beat the other team! I have to give a Hats off to the other team because they were tough to beat. We all got Ice Cream after and enjoyed victory!

  • Implemented Online Attendence Management System

    1. Accuracy Humans are prone to errors, even if we provide them with supporting computional devices. Automated attendence management systems ensure accurate time records and minimize the inevitable and costly errors with manual data entry. This accurate data thereby helps to provide accurate performance and payroll data. 2. Economics Have you ever thought that attendence management systems could control costs? It saves money by putting an end to inaccurate time reporting, buddy punching, absenteelsm, tardiness, time abuse, and overpayment. As a matter of fact, a study done by the Axisum Group found that large organizations save up to $1,600 per employee after implementing automated attendence management systems. 3. Productivity/ Effecienty Monitoring and managing attendence manually can be time consuming, laborious, and expensive affair. It takes time to process paper sheets and time cards, create schedules, authorize leave and overtime, and create payroll manually. Free up precious time administration time with automated system that does it all for you, from keeping track of employee hours to automatically importing information into your payroll system. The time and effort saved combined with data accuracy helps in optimizing the use of resources which lead to increased productivity and improves profits.
